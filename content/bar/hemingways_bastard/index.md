My favorite riff of the classic daiquiri. Often a go-to of mine for a gin base.

## Recipe

- 2ozÂ (60mL) Rum
- 0.5oz (15mL) Campari
- 0.5oz (15mL) Luxardo Maraschino
- 0.75oz (45mL) lime juice
- 0.5oz (15mL) Demerara

## Instructions

1. Combine ingredients into a shaker
2. Strain and pour
3. Enjoy
